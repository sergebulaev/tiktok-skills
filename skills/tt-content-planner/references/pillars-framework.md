# The TikTok Pillar Framework

Content pillars are the 3 to 4 recurring themes your account posts under. They
keep a feed coherent (which trains the ranker on who to show you to) and they
make planning a week a fill-in-the-blank exercise instead of a blank-page panic.

## The default mix

For most creators building a niche:

| Pillar | Share | Job |
|---|---|---|
| **Teach** | 40% | the value that earns saves and follows: tutorials, numbers, lists |
| **Story** | 30% | the human pull that earns completion and shares: real moments, tension |
| **Trend** | 20% | the reach lever: trending sounds bent to your niche |
| **Promotion** | 10% | your product, service, or offer, framed as a result |

Adjust by goal: a creator who needs reach fast leans Trend higher for a few weeks;
a creator monetizing leans Promotion to 15-20% but never more, or trust erodes.

## Why a clear niche raises the floor

TikTok's ranker learns who to show your videos to from the pattern of your posts.
A scattered account (cooking one day, finance the next, a dance the third) gives
the ranker no signal, so every video starts cold. A tight niche means each new
post inherits an audience the ranker already mapped. Pillars enforce the niche
without making every video identical.

## How pillars map to formulas

| Pillar | Preferred hook formulas | Primary goals |
|---|---|---|
| Teach | T9 Tutorial Cold Start, T3 Number Reveal, T7 Listicle Promise | saves, follows |
| Story | T8 Story In Medias Res, T5 Relatable Call-Out | completion, shares |
| Trend | T10 Trend-Ride, T2 Pattern Interrupt | shares, reach |
| Promotion | T1 Cold-Open Result | completion, conversion |

## Pillar rules

1. **No pillar over 60% of the week.** A feed that is all teaching reads as a
   textbook; all story reads as a vlog. The mix is the point.
2. **Teach earns the follow; Story earns the watch.** A week needs both. Pure
   value with no personality does not build a following; pure personality with no
   value does not get saved.
3. **Trend is seasoning.** 1-2 trend rides a week. Over-relying on trends means
   you borrow reach but never build a niche of your own.
4. **Promotion is a result, not an ad.** The Promotion pillar still uses a hook
   formula (usually T1 Cold-Open Result). "Here is what I built" beats "buy my
   thing".

## Building your own pillars

If the default mix does not fit, derive pillars from three questions:

1. **What do you know that your audience wants?** -> your Teach pillar.
2. **What have you actually lived in this niche?** -> your Story pillar.
3. **What do you sell or want to be known for?** -> your Promotion pillar.

Trend is universal: it is the same lever for every niche, applied through
`tt-trend-mapper`.

## Weekly discipline

- Assign every planned video to exactly one pillar.
- Check the mix against the percentages before you film.
- If one pillar is starving (no Story all week), the feed will feel flat. Rebalance
  before the batch shoot, not after.
