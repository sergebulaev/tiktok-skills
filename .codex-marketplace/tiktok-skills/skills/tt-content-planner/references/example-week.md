## Hook-batching schedule

Two batch sessions keep filming sane:

**Batch A (Sunday before the week) - desk setup, same framing:**
- Mon T9 hook (git reflog result on screen)
- Wed T3 hook ("80% in 3 files" big on screen)
- Sat T7 hook (counter "1 / 5")
- Sun T1 hook (the side project result in frame one)

These four share a desk, lighting, and outfit, so film the openers back to back.

**Batch B (film day-of) - flexible:**
- Tue T8 story (needs a different energy; film the night you tell it)
- Thu T10 trend (film close to posting, the sound window moves)
- Fri T5 relatable (a quick re-enactment, film morning-of)
